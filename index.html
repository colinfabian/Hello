Top 10 Bills Players of All Time

1. Jim Kelly (QB)

  • 5× Pro Bowl selection
  • 4× AFC Champion (1990–1993)
  • Buffalo Bills Wall of Fame
  • Pro Football Hall of Fame (2002)
Kelly was the heart and soul of the Bills' greatest era, leading the team to four straight Super Bowls. His leadership, toughness, and command of the K-Gun offense made him one of the most influential quarterbacks of his generation.

2. Bruce Smith (DE)

  • 11× Pro Bowl selection
  • 2× NFL Defensive Player of the Year
  • All-time NFL sack leader (200 sacks)
  • Pro Football Hall of Fame (2009)
Smith is the NFL's all-time sack leader and a dominant force on defense. His relentless pass rush and consistency made him a nightmare for opposing offenses and a cornerstone of the Bills' defense for nearly two decades.

3. Thurman Thomas (RB)

  • 5× Pro Bowl selection
  • 1991 NFL MVP
  • Buffalo Bills all-time leading rusher
  • Pro Football Hall of Fame (2007)
Thomas was the engine of the Bills' offense, excelling as both a runner and receiver. His versatility and production were key to the team's sustained success in the early 1990s.

4. Andre Reed (WR)

  • 7× Pro Bowl selection
  • 951 career receptions
  • Buffalo Bills all-time leading receiver
  • Pro Football Hall of Fame (2014)
Reed was Kelly's favorite target and a model of consistency. His toughness and ability to make big plays in crucial moments made him one of the best receivers of his era.

5. O.J. Simpson (RB)

  • 6× Pro Bowl selection
  • 1973 NFL MVP
  • First player to rush for over 2,000 yards in a season
  • Pro Football Hall of Fame (1985)
Simpson was one of the most electrifying runners in NFL history. His 2,003-yard season in 1973 remains one of the greatest single-season performances ever.

6. Steve Tasker (ST/WR)

  • 7× Pro Bowl selection (as special teamer)
  • 5× First-team All-Pro
  • Buffalo Bills Wall of Fame
Tasker is widely regarded as the best special teams player in NFL history. His impact on field position and ability to make game-changing plays were invaluable to the Bills' success.

7. Fred Jackson (RB)

  • Over 5,000 career rushing yards
  • Buffalo Bills Wall of Fame
  • Fan favorite and team captain
Jackson's journey from undrafted free agent to team captain is a testament to his work ethic and determination. He was a consistent producer and a leader on and off the field.

8. Cornelius Bennett (LB)

  • 5× Pro Bowl selection
  • 3× First-team All-Pro
  • Buffalo Bills Wall of Fame
Bennett was a dominant linebacker and a key part of the Bills' Super Bowl defenses. His athleticism and playmaking ability set the tone for the defense.

9. Eric Moulds (WR)

  • 3× Pro Bowl selection
  • 2× 1,000+ yard seasons
  • Buffalo Bills Wall of Fame
Moulds was one of the most productive receivers in team history, known for his big-play ability and consistency during a challenging era for the franchise.

10. Kyle Williams (DT)

  • 6× Pro Bowl selection
  • Buffalo Bills Wall of Fame
  • Team captain and locker room leader
Williams was the heart of the Bills' defense for over a decade. His leadership, toughness, and production made him a fan favorite and a respected figure in the locker room.
